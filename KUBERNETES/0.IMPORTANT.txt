which is better cka or ckad


Neither CKA nor CKAD is strictly "better," as they target different roles: CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator) is superior for infrastructure, troubleshooting, and DevOps roles, while CKAD (Certified Kubernetes Application Developer) is better for app developers focusing on building and deploying containerized applications.
